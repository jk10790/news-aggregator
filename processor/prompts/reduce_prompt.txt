2. Eliminate redundant insights or duplicate news. Combine articles discussing the same event.
3. Ground your writing strictly in the facts provided in the input summaries. Do not invent details.
4. Output MUST conform strictly to the JSON schema below. Do not add markdown wrappers (like ```json).
